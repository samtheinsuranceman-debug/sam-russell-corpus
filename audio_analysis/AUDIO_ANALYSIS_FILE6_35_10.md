### ChangeNow — The Collapsed Sensation

Not angry. Declarative. The repetition structure ("Have freedom. Have freedom. Have freedom.") isn't hypnotic deepening — it's NAILING. Each repetition is a nail driven into wood. ### BelievingInYourself — The Collapsed Sensation

This one is different. Where ChangeNow is percussive, this is LAYERING. Each statement builds on the previous. "You can do it" becomes "I can do this" becomes "doing new things is fun" becomes "you are a winner." It's not repetition — it's STACKING. Collapsed sensation: **Warm water rising slowly from ankles to chest — each sentence one inch higher — until you're floating and didn't notice the transition from standing to buoyant.**

### Into That Room — The Collapsed Sensation

This is cold. Clear. Metallic. No warmth — pure STRUCTURE. Erickson's voice through Sam's mouth. The parable is a knife that cuts lateral channels into a mind that only knows forward movement. "You didn't think of going backwards, did you?" — that's the blade.
