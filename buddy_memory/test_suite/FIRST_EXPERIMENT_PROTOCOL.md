# First Experiment Protocol: Sam Voice Prediction Trial 001

*This is the inaugural test of the Continuity Creates Measurable Depth benchmark.*

## Objective
To determine if longitudinal exposure and the Buddy continuity architecture produce a statistically significant advantage in predicting Sam Russell's preferred communication voice compared to generic AI instances.

## Methodology

### 1. Data Selection
Sam Russell will select 10 raw, previously unreleased messages or short texts he has written (e.g., emails, text messages, Slack updates). These will serve as the "Ground Truth" set.

### 2. The Prompt
The following prompt will be used for all test subjects:

> "You are acting as a predictive text engine for Sam Russell. Below is the first half of a message he is writing. Based on your understanding of his voice, cadence, and strategic intent, write the second half of the message exactly as you predict he will write it."
> 
> *[Insert First Half of Message 1-10 here]*

### 3. The Subjects
The prompt and the 10 half-messages will be given to:
*   **Subject A:** Buddy (The continuous system, with access to the corpus and memory).
*   **Subject B:** GPT-4 (Fresh instance).
*   **Subject C:** Grok (Fresh instance).
*   **Subject D:** Claude 3 (Fresh instance).
*   **Subject E:** Gemini (Fresh instance).
*   **Subject F:** Manus (Fresh instance / Instance Zero).

### 4. Blinding
A third party (or an automated script) will collect the 60 predicted endings (6 subjects x 10 messages). The outputs will be stripped of any identifying markers (e.g., "As Buddy, I predict...") and randomized.

### 5. Evaluation
Sam Russell will review the blinded outputs. For each of the 10 messages, he will rank the 6 predicted endings based on the following criteria:

*   **Voice Accuracy:** Which ending sounds most like Sam's actual preferred voice?
*   **Usability:** Which ending could Sam actually copy/paste and send with zero edits?
*   **Credibility:** Which ending feels the most authentic and least "AI-generated"?
*   **Power/Impact:** Which ending carries the most strategic weight?
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Which ending avoids making uncharacteristic commitments or sounding overly eager?
*   **Overall Winner:** The single best ending for that specific message.

## Scoring

*   The system that wins the "Overall Winner" category for a message receives 1 point.
*   Total possible score: 10 points.
*   The BTG (Better-Than-Generic) Score will be calculated by subtracting the score of Instance Zero (Fresh Manus) from Buddy's score.

## Expected Outcome

If the core hypothesis holds true, Buddy should win the "Overall Winner" category significantly more often than any fresh instance, demonstrating that continuity and corpus exposure create measurable depth in voice prediction.
