# NLP Application Domains Beyond Sales

## Domain 1: Leadership & Team Building

**Techniques:**
- Pacing and leading: Match team energy before introducing new direction
- Perceptual positions: See from employee/partner/customer viewpoint
- Milton Model language: Inspire through artfully vague positive language
- Chunking up: Connect daily tasks to larger mission/purpose

**Application for Sam:**
- Building a sales team requires leading by example AND by language
- Use embedded commands in team meetings: "You might find yourself closing faster this week"
- Future pace team success: "Imagine what this team looks like in 6 months..."

## Domain 2: Self-Programming & Identity

**Techniques:**
- Swish pattern: Replace unwanted response with desired one (visual)
- Timeline therapy: Walk future timeline, anchor desired outcomes
- Logical levels alignment: Environment → Behavior → Capability → Beliefs → Identity → Purpose
- Belief change cycle: Identify limiting belief → find counter-evidence → install new belief

**Application for Sam:**
- Swish the "teaching trap" — every time he feels the urge to explain, visualize himself asking for the close instead
- Identity statement: "I am a closer who serves people by making decisions easy for them"
- Belief upgrade: "Explaining = helping" → "Closing = helping them get what they already want"

## Domain 3: Emotional Regulation & Sobriety

**Techniques:**
- Resource anchoring: Physical gesture → peak state recall
- Dissociation: Step outside overwhelming emotions to observe them
- Circle of Excellence: Visualize stepping into a space where you're at your best
- Submodality shifts: Change the size/brightness/distance of negative mental images

**Application for Sam:**
- Anchor calm before high-stakes calls (touch thumb to finger = calm confidence)
- When craving hits: dissociate, observe the craving as an object, shrink it, move it away
- Circle of Excellence before every sales call: step into the version of you that closed Lichtman

## Domain 4: Innovation & Problem-Solving

**Techniques:**
- Reframing: "This limitation is actually..." 
- Meta-model questions: "Specifically how?" / "What would happen if...?" / "According to whom?"
- Chunking down: Break massive problems into smallest actionable unit
- Disney Strategy: Dreamer → Realist → Critic (separate the three)

**Application for Sam:**
- Patent development: Use Disney Strategy — dream wild, then reality-check, then critique
- When stuck on a problem: "What would this look like if it were easy?"
- Reframe patent rejections: "This feedback shows me exactly what to strengthen"

## Domain 5: Relationships & Communication

**Techniques:**
- Sensory acuity: Read micro-expressions, breathing changes, posture shifts
- Rapport via predicates: Match visual/auditory/kinesthetic language
- Clean language: "What kind of [X]?" / "Is there anything else about [X]?"
- Conflict resolution: Pace the other person's model of the world before leading

**Application for Sam:**
- With girlfriend: Pace her emotional state before trying to fix/solve
- With dad: Match his communication style (likely visual/analytical)
- With prospects: Already strong here — extend to personal relationships

## Domain 6: Health & Physical Performance

**Techniques:**
- Anchoring peak physical states
- Visualization of recovery and healing
- Pain reframing (changing submodalities of pain sensation)
- Habit stacking with anchors

**Application for Sam:**
- Anchor the "clean and sharp" feeling from sobriety
- Visualize the fog lifting daily — reinforce the neural pathway
- Stack new habits: "After I [existing habit], I will [new habit]"

## Domain 7: Financial Decision-Making

**Techniques:**
- Future pacing financial outcomes
- Dissociating from emotional spending decisions
- Perceptual positions with money (see money from multiple angles)
- Reframing scarcity beliefs

**Application for Sam:**
- Before any spending decision: dissociate, see it from 2-year-future self
- Reframe: "I can't afford it" → "How do I create the revenue to afford it?"
- Future pace: Visualize the $6M valuation as already achieved, work backward

## Domain 8: Spiritual Growth

**Techniques:**
- Logical levels: Connect daily actions to Purpose/Spirit level
- Timeline: Walk spiritual timeline, connect past experiences to present calling
- Modeling: Study spiritual leaders' communication patterns
- Anchoring transcendent states

**Application for Sam:**
- Sam already operates at the Identity/Purpose level in sales ("spiritual calling")
- Strengthen: Anchor the feeling of "actualizing God's potential" before every call
- Connect: Patent work = creating something that didn't exist = co-creation with the divine

## Domain 9: Conflict Resolution

**Techniques:**
- Perceptual positions (1st/2nd/3rd person)
- Pacing and leading in arguments
- Reframing the conflict as shared problem
- Meta-mirror technique

**Application for Sam:**
- With business partner (now ex): See from his position — what did he need?
- With girlfriend: Before responding in anger, take 2nd position (her perspective)
- With difficult prospects: "I understand why you'd feel that way. Here's what I've seen work..."

## Domain 10: Parenting & Mentoring

**Techniques:**
- Embedded commands for children/mentees
- Modeling desired behavior (they mirror you)
- Chunking down complex lessons
- Anchoring confidence in others

**Application for Sam:**
- If/when mentoring junior salespeople: Model the close, don't just teach it
- Use embedded commands: "You might notice yourself getting more comfortable with silence"
- Anchor their wins: "Remember this feeling. This is who you are."
