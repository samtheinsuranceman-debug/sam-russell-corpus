**I tracked every "buying signal" across 52 sales calls. Here are the top 5 signals advisors miss:**

2. **The spouse mention.** "I'd need to talk to my wife/husband" is NOT an objection. It's a buying signal wrapped in a permission request. They're asking you to help them sell it at home.

5. **The silence after the close.** Most advisors fill this silence. DON'T. The silence IS the decision happening. Every word you add resets the clock.
